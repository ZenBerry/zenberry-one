  • Some of the commands:
    • Guitar chord player. Syntax: >chord 2 2 2 0 x x
    • Midi notes player. Syntax: >play 80 90 2x
    • Timer. Syntax: >timer 10
    • Stopwatch. Syntax: >stw
    • Ear training. Syntax: >ear
    • Audio Recorder. Syntax: >rec
    • Find a node. Syntax: >find hello
    • Jump to a node. Syntax: >goto hello
    • Show a raw node code. Syntax: >raw
    • Change node's kind. Syntax: >kind html
